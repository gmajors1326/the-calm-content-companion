🧭 The Companion Suite: Member Guide

Welcome to your creative sanctuary. These tools are designed to remove the noise and help you build high-authority content with zero friction.

1. How to Use the Tools

Every tool follows a simple Input → Architecture → Asset flow:

  • 🌿 Input: Paste a messy thought, a rough draft, or a simple topic.
  • 🌿 Generate: Click the button to let the AI analyze your "Creative DNA."
  • 🌿 Result: You will receive a structured breakdown. Each generation counts as one run.

2. Understanding Your Runs

Your creative capacity is measured in Monthly Runs.

  • 🌿 The Process: Every time you hit "Generate Clarity," 1 run is deducted from your monthly total.
  • 🌿 Reset: Your runs refresh automatically on the 1st of every month.
  • 🌿 Check Your Balance: You can always see your remaining runs in the top-right corner of your Dashboard.

3. Troubleshooting & FAQs

Question Answer
Why is the "Generate" button grey? You have either reached your monthly limit of runs or the text box is empty.
Can I save my results? Currently, results are "live." We recommend copying your favorite generations into a notes app immediately.
How do I get more runs? Click the "Upgrade" link next to your run counter to see the Starter, Pro, and Elite options.
